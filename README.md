# Dairy-Dataset-Analysis  

## Dataset Overview  
  - The dataset focuses on dairy product sales, inventory management, production analysis, customer demand, pricing, shelf life, and profitability. The SQL queries are used to extract business insights such as revenue trends, stock management, product performance, customer purchasing behaviour, and operational efficiency.
  - The project demonstrates how SQL can be applied in real-world business scenarios to support decision-making in the dairy industry.

## Dataset Description  
  -  It includes information about different dairy products such as milk, butter, cheese, yogurt, and other dairy items.
  -  Product-level information includes product name, brand, category, listed price, selling price, stock quantity, and reorder details.
  -  Inventory-related fields help track stock levels, minimum stock thresholds, and products requiring immediate restocking.
  -  Shelf life data is included to monitor product expiration and identify items with low remaining shelf life.
  -  Customer location data helps analyze regional purchasing patterns and identify top-performing markets.

## Tools & Features used  
  -  SQL
  -  Excel Datasets
  - Microsoft Word Documentation
  - Aggregate functions, group by, order by
  - Where clause for the conditions

## Key Insights and Findings  
  -  The curd product has the highest revenue among all the other products.
  -  Amul brand is considered as the strongest performing among all the brands.
  -  The most of the sales of the business is done in the retail channel.
  -  The milk is considered as the most profitable product for the business.
  -  The state Tamil Nadu has produce highest quantity among the others state.

## Conclusion  
This project successfully demonstrates how SQL can be used as a powerful analytical tool to extract meaningful business insights from a dairy industry dataset. By applying SQL queries to analyze sales, revenue, inventory, customer demand, pricing, shelf life, production, and profitability, the project converts raw data into actionable business information
